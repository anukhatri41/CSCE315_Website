H-E-B Project

During my internship at H-E-B this past summer, I had the opportunity to work on a project under the Cart to Home Team at H-E-B Digital. I worked on moving the Time Slot Picker (used in choosing a time slot for curbside/delivery of groceries) from Vue to React. I connected the component to real data through GraphQL queries and mutations that I made. I used Apollo to connect the GraphQL to React. I also updated the UI for the Time Slot Picker to match the newer design that other pages on H-E-B have. Those were all viewed in Storybook. It was difficult to do a remote internship, but I learned a lot in eight short weeks. I enjoyed working on a project that I know will soon be live and in production since the Time Slot Picker is a vital part of H-E-B’s eCommerce, especially since COVID has increased the demand for curbside and delivery services.

TAMUhack 2020

I participated in A&M’s hackathon in January 2020. I made a website called Plane Sleep, which provided users with tips on how to relax on a plane or to even sleep on a plane. It was geared for those with the fear of flying and new travelers. It was my first time making a website from scratch using HTML and CSS. I used Firebase to host the site. For a 24-hour project, I am proud of the turnout of the design and content, especially since I made it by myself where as other projects were made by teams.
